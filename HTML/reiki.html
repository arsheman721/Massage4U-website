Reiki

What is Reiki healing?

Reiki is a powerful ancient healing technique and means Universal Life Force. By working 'with hands' on or off the body and channelling the Universal Life Force, profound healing can be achieved. This Healing Energy helps to release stress, tension, aches and pains.

The word reiki consists of two Japanese words. As it is difficult to translate the meaning of Japanese words into English precisely, and there are different meanings given for these words:
Rei – Universal energy, Universal life force that creates, surrounds and sustains matter, the Source of creation or God’s highest power
Ki – The personalised form of the Universal energy, the life force within us, energy inside and surrounding all living things, also called Chi, Qi and Prana.

In a perfectly healthy person, the Ki will flow freely throughout the body and the electro-magnetic field. When the flow of Ki is impeded by blockages, energy will stagnate and illness and disease will occur. Reiki works to clear any blockages so that Ki can once again flow freely and health can be restored. This is the basis of many philosophies and healing systems throughout the world, such as acupuncture and reflexology.

What are the benefits?
Reiki is beneficial for all levels of healing from physical illness and disease, to mental and emotional conflicts. At various times in peoples’ lives they may also need extra help to reduce stress, enhance sleeping habits, and heal relationships. By taking advantage of Reiki healing energy to aid quality of life, it is possible to avoid some of the more serious illnesses brought on by stress and negative energies.

What are the side effects?
There are no documented side effects following a treatment. However, both the recipient and the practitioner may feel tired for a short while. It is possible that the receiver may experience good sensations such as:

  • An energy surge
  • A relaxed feeling
  • A sensation of being comforted and/or pampered

What to do after a treatment?

  • Following a treatment it is important to remain seated for a few minutes to allow any side effects to wear off.
  • It is important to drink plenty of water or herbal tea to help the toxins to be flushed out of the system.
  • It is also important not to eat a heavy meal immediately following the treatment – if the receiver is hungry then a light meal only is advised.
  • The receiver should take care if driving home afterwards as they could feel very relaxed!
  • All stimulants, such as caffeine, alcohol and cigarettes should be avoided.
  • If the receiver suffers any skin irritations following the treatment then a shower is advised.
  • The receiver should allow five to ten days between treatments to give the body a chance to get rid of the toxins liberated by the treatment.

Where and when can it be performed?

During a treatment, the receiver generally lies comfortably on a massage table and remains fully-clothed throughout. The treatment can be carried out in the practitioner’s environment or in the receiver’s own home. It can also be done via distance healing at times pre-arranged between the receiver and the practitioner.
Many people like to have soft, relaxing music playing to help them let go of their daily stresses. A treatment can be wonderfully relaxing and a very enjoyable experience.

Different individuals will feel the Reiki energy differently during a treatment. Some people feel the energy as a gentle heat, some feel a coolness, and others may feel a pleasant tingling sensation. All the sensations felt by people are perfectly normal and natural. Some people feel nothing at all. As with the other sensations that may be felt during a Reiki treatment, feeling nothing is also perfectly normal. You can rest assured that Reiki will be working to help you regardless of what you physically feel.

Reiki is not something that requires a belief or faith in anything, whether it is a religion or in Reiki itself. The Reiki energy works regardless of religious beliefs and faith and Reiki will also work if the receiver is sceptical or doesn't believe in Reiki.

How long does a treatment last?

An average treatment will take between 45 – 60 minutes. The initial consultation, done on the first treatment, will take about 30 minutes. The consultation also helps us to look at the cause of the problem in a holistic way by looking deeper at the potential causes of the condition.
